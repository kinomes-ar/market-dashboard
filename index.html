  • Trump: "the ceasefire is over" — the Dow fell -1.09% (-576.76 pts to 52,348.39) as geopolitics shook the tape again. After US forces hit Iran "very hard" late Tuesday in retaliation for attacks on three commercial vessels in the Strait of Hormuz, Trump declared the truce dead at the NATO summit. The S&P 500 sank as much as -1.1% intraday before closing -0.28% (7,482.71); the Nasdaq rose +0.20% (25,870.65) for a mixed finish.

After the dot plot showed 9 of 18 officials expecting a 2026 hike, the minutes revealed a committee split between inflation falling to 2% on its own and staying sticky. Chair Warsh kept his no-dots, no-forward-guidance stance (a 130-word statement). Sep-hike odds ~58%.
