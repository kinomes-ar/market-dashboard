읽는 법Takeaway

어제와 오늘은 정확히 반대 방향의 하루다. 유가 -2%가 '유가발 인플레' 공포를 한 발 물렸고, 그 빈자리를 마이크론 $250B와 SK하이닉스 7배 청약이 채우며 메모리 사이클 서사가 주도권을 되찾았다. 다만 구조는 그대로다 — 시장은 여전히 호르무즈(유가→물가→인상 확률)와 메모리 사이클이라는 두 개의 시계를 본다. 내일 SK하이닉스의 데뷔 가격이 이 반등이 단순 되돌림인지 랠리 재개인지를 말해줄 첫 데이터다.

Today was yesterday in reverse. Oil's -2% pullback pushed the oil-inflation scare back a step, and Micron's $250B plus SK Hynix's 7x book filled the void — the memory-cycle story took the wheel back. The structure hasn't changed, though: the market still watches two clocks, Hormuz (oil → inflation → hike odds) and the memory cycle. Tomorrow's SK Hynix debut print is the first data point on whether this bounce is a mere retracement or the rally resuming.

A rebound, but a low-quality one: an opening +3.3% shrank to +0.62% and the index was briefly pressed toward 7,000 intraday — bargain-hunting and profit-taking fighting to a near-draw. Retail is using strength to exit, and the upside is concentrated in the Hynix ADR story, so tomorrow's (Jul 10) Nasdaq debut is the key to near-term direction. A 7x-oversubscribed offering is clearly positive, but a "sell-the-news" reaction after listing is equally possible. The next checkpoint is the Jul 16 BOK meeting; until then, assume the wide-swing volatility regime continues.
