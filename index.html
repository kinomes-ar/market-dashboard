읽는 법Takeaway

핵심은 '중동 긴장 완화·유가 급락이 만든 안도 랠리'다. 지난주 5거래일 연속 하락하던 나스닥이 +2%대로 강하게 되돌림하고 다우가 사상 첫 5만2천을 넘긴 것은, 위험회피의 주범이던 지정학·에너지 변수가 한 풀 꺾였음을 보여준다. 버라이즌을 대체해 다우에 편입된 알파벳이 +5% 급등하며 빅테크 투자심리가 회복됐고, 칩 조정도 일단 진정 국면. 다만 반등의 천장은 여전히 매파 연준이다. 기준금리 3.50–3.75% 동결 속 에너지·관세발 인플레 고착 우려가 남아, 시장은 연내 추가 완화에 신중하다. 결국 7/2 6월 고용이 분수령 — 고용이 강하면 금리 인상 우려가 재부각되며 변동성이 되살아날 수 있고, 둔화 신호면 안도 랠리가 연장될 공산이다.

The key is a relief rally born of calmer Middle East tension and a slide in oil. The Nasdaq — down five straight last week — snapped back over +2% and the Dow cleared 52,000 for the first time, a sign the geopolitical and energy drivers behind the recent risk-off have eased. Alphabet, newly added to the Dow in place of Verizon, jumped ~5% and restored big-tech sentiment, while the chip correction steadied for now. Yet the ceiling on the rebound is still a hawkish Fed. With rates on hold at 3.50–3.75% and energy- and tariff-driven inflation still sticky, markets stay cautious on further easing this year. So June payrolls (Jul 2) is the pivot — a hot print could revive rate-hike fears and volatility, while a soft one would likely extend the relief rally.

An extreme decoupling opened up within one market — large-cap chips (KOSPI) fell -2.05% while neglected small-caps (KOSDAQ) soared +8.13%. The driver was the unwind of the 'Samsung-nix' concentration, with money rotating violently into stocks that had been crushed to pandemic-crash levels. Foreign megacap selling and a softer won (~1,540) still cap the KOSPI. Whether it lasts hinges on chip dumping calming, the Jul 16 BOK meeting and TSMC results, and June trade data on Jul 1.
